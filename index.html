The villagers' voices

The voices of the villagers took us quite some time. We had been told by the development team that there would be much dialogue with them in the game. They chose not to have these dialogues dubbed, and we were asked to find an alternative way to make the villagers speak.
Once we had identified the type of voice we would like to propose, we looked for software that would allow us to generate voices with realistic diction; during this research we found out Soundly had recently introduced a new feature called "voices," and it turned out to be a very functional tool for our purposes.
After several tests, we figured out how to most effectively control the expression of voices, even achieving different intonations and emotions.
The final step was organizing a single FMOD event that would work for all dialogues with the villagers, with control over the tone and duration.
